SOME GAMES TO TRY

CALL OF DUTY

Call of Duty is the best! It's so cool how you can run around and shoot stuff. My favorite part is when you get to use the big sniper rifle and zoom in super close. It's like you're in a movie, but you get to play it. I always play with my friends, and we try to see who can get the most kills. It's so much fun!


FREE FIRE

Free Fire is the best because it's so easy to play, even on my small phone. The games are super fast, so I can play a bunch of rounds during break time at school. I love getting cool new character skins, and it's awesome when you get a booyah! I always play with my friends, and it's fun to see who can get the most kills. Plus, you get to parachute out of a plane, and that's just so nice!


MORTAL KOMBAT

Mortal Kombat is so cool because you get to do a lot of crazy moves and combos. My favorite part is the Fatalities, they are so funny and gory. I always play as Scorpion because he can throw a rope and pull the other player close. My friends and I always fight each other to see who can get the best moves. It's the best!


BURN OUT DOMINATOR

Burnout Dominator is so fun! It's like, a super fast race, but you get to crash into all the other cars. My favorite part is when you drive really crazy and your boost turns blue, so you can go even faster! It's so cool how you can smash other cars into walls to make a shortcut. And the music is so good! It's the best racing game ever!


STREETHD RACING

Street Racing HD is awesome because you get to race with your friends and crash into them! The cars are so cool and you can even make them look the way you want with different paint colors and stickers. I love how you get to drift around corners and get a nitro boost to go super fast! The tracks are amazing, and it's so fun to beat everyone and win the final crown. It's the best!
